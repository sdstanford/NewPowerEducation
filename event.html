Women Make a Difference: How to Get a Seat at the Table

November 2, 2019

On Saturday, November 2nd, 2019 NewPower Education hosted Women Make a Difference: How to Get a Seat at the Table, an exciting day-long workshop for women interested in engaging in public service in Georgia. Attendees learned from inspiring women speakers currently serving in city, county and state government, and made valuable connections with their fellow participants.

Andrea Young, Executive Director of ACLU-Georgia, delivered a special keynote address. Other speakers included State Representative Park Cannon, The Honorable LaTisha Dear Jackson, DeKalb County Superior Court, Council President Felicia Moore, Atlanta City Council, and Commissioner Lisa Cupid, Cobb County.
